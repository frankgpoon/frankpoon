Flashcard Study Buddy

about

Flashcard Study Buddy is an interactive voice-powered learning tool powered by Quizlet, created by my friend Michael Sulistio and me during summer 2017. It allows users to study their Quizlet flash cards verbally much like with an actual buddy.

To try out Flashcard Study Buddy, just say to your Google Assistant on any platform, such as Allo, Google Home, Android, and the Google Assistant app:

Talk to Flashcard Study Buddy

For more information, including more detailed instructions and screenshots, check out Michael's page for Flashcard Study Buddy.
