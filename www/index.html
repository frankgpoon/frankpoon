Hi, I'm Frank. I am a third year student at the University of Washington studying computer science. This quarter, I am also a teaching assistant for CSE 331.

At this company, I initially focused on researching chatbots for their services based on my experience with projects like Flashcard Study Buddy, but moved on to building modules that added to the main backend infrastructure, with a short period building data sets.
