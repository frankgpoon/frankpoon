Instructions

The StyleCheck tool is meant to check for internal correctness as defined by the University of Washington Department of Computer Science and Engineering. This tool does not guarantee full style points. Use at your own risk. I take no responsibility for points lost through using this tool.

Before uploading your source code, make sure it compiles. It should be have the extension .java and be under 16kb. You can choose what style errors you want specifically to check. Current supported errors are: Name Consistency, Encapsulation, Line Length, and Boolean Zen.

I will be updating this tool with more features when I have time.
